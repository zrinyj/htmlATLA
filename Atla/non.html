Nonbending,

not everyone is fortunate to be given bending. Even though it is normal in our world, it is the opposite in the Avatar the Last Airbender's world. People who cannot bend tend to be lower class citizens and tend to be the forgetten people. Obviously not everyone, who cannot bend, is forgotten as some learn to fight and utilise their fighting techniques to outmatch those who bend. Some strong fighters include Suki, Sokka, and Ty Lee. Suki and Sokka are extremely skilled fighters who commonly use fans and swords to protect and attack. Ty Lee uses her knowledge of chi blocking to gain the upperhand on her opponents. This form usually leaves the opponent paralyzed or unable to bend for a while.

Although this group are not all the fighting type. One should never underestimate these fighters.
